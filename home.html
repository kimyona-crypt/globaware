OUR MISSION

Our goals

To conserve and protect natural resources for future generations, and to protect human health through environmental stewardship. To aware people about global warming and means to avoid it. To encourage the crowd to work for the greenery and safety of the society. Our website not only shows the problems faced across the world but also showcases different means to protect as well as the events being held in your vicinity.

Our Vision

A world where natural resources are equitably available, and all children are healthier as a result of living the principles of the Go Green Initiative.
Establish a place where there is no fear of water getting scarce, weather becoming unpredictable, places becoming unoccupiable. So it became the mission for us, that as our Organization grows it will make proportionate amount of good impact on the environment.

Our Mission

 protect biodiversity in all its forms
 prevent pollution and abuse of the earth’s ocean, land, air and fresh water
 end all nuclear threats
 promote peace, global disarmament and non-violence
